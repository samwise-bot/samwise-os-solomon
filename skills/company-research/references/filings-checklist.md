# Filings & Statements Checklist

Use latest available: 10-K, 10-Q, most recent 8-K earnings release, and earnings call transcript if available.

## Extract fast
- Revenue: YoY/QoQ growth, organic vs acquisition-driven if disclosed
- Margins: gross, operating, net trend and drivers
- Cash quality: CFO, FCF, SBC intensity, working-capital swings
- Balance sheet: debt maturities, net debt/cash, liquidity runway
- Guidance: raised/maintained/cut + tone shift vs prior quarter
- Segment health: growth and margin by segment/geography
- Risk factors: new/expanded risk language and litigation/regulatory updates

## Red flags
- Growth from one-offs, not core demand
- Margin expansion only from temporary cost cuts
- CFO diverging from earnings repeatedly
- Inventory/receivables mismatch with stated demand
- Guidance language deterioration while headline numbers look fine
