# Trading Non-Negotiables (Ian)

_Last updated: 2026-02-27_

1. **Objective:** Aim to outperform S&P 500 YoY via disciplined process (no guarantees).
2. **Universe:** US stocks only; **no ETFs**, **no penny stocks**.
3. **Directionality:** **Long-only** (no shorting).
4. **Turnover bias:** Keep positions when thesis remains intact (tax-aware, lower churn).
5. **Research standard:** Macro regime first, then EDGAR/fundamentals/news/sentiment synthesis.
6. **Selection standard:** Aggressive filtering; only high-conviction setups.
7. **Output standard:** Ranked picks with clear thesis + explicit entry/exit levels (stop-loss & take-profit).
8. **Process control:** Track a $100 paper portfolio daily.
9. **Adaptation:** Full RCCA per trade (Root Cause & Corrective Action) with rule updates.
10. **Persistence:** Log requirements/changes to memory so they survive resets.
